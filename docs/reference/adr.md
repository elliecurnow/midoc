# Administrative data

A simulated dataset

## Usage

``` r
adr
```

## Format

### `adr`

A data frame with 1000 rows and 5 columns:

- gcse_score:

  Child's GCSE score (at approximately age 16 years), as a percentage,
  calculated from the total of an individualâ€™s top eight GCSE
  qualifications, ranked in terms of points

- log_income:

  Logarithm of the family income per annum, in UK pounds, when the child
  is aged 5 years

- mated:

  Mother's educational level: post-16 years qualification or not

- ks2_score:

  Child's Key Stage 2 score (at approximately age 11 years), as a
  percentage, calculated from the total of an individual's English,
  maths and science scores

- r_log_income:

  Missingness indicator: whether log_income is reported or not
